# Ace Scout — Platonic Vision for hypothesisdriven.org

**THE IDEAL**: A site that makes a tenure committee member think "this person is serious, productive, and collaborative" within 10 seconds. Every element serves one of four audiences: dept chair, collaborator, journalist, grant reviewer. The site IS the tenure argument — not a brochure, an evidence map.

## Fully Realized State

- **Big Questions are the spine.** Each Q links DOWN to its publications (grouped by research arc: Phenotype / Mechanism / Translation) and FORWARD to its Next Question. A reviewer can trace: question → approach → result → paper → next question. The intellectual trajectory is self-evident.
- **Print stylesheet** makes the page a ready-made tenure packet insert. Committee members print; we optimize for that.
- **PI card** shows funding signal + pub count. Thin team reads as "small lab" — the card compensates with grant-line credibility.
- **Contact form is live**, pushing to Jeff's phone in real time.
- **Every image** has a real figure or is honestly absent. No placeholder theater.
