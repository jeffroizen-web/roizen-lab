Our Research

Research

The Roizen Lab investigates the diverse biological roles of vitamin D beyond its classical functions in calcium and bone metabolism. Our work aims to uncover how vitamin D signaling influences a wide range of physiological processes and disease states.

Immune Modulation

Investigating how vitamin D regulates immune cell function and its implications for autoimmune disease, infection susceptibility, and inflammatory disorders.

Cardiovascular Health

Examining the role of vitamin D signaling in vascular function, blood pressure regulation, and cardiovascular disease risk.

Metabolic Regulation

Studying the effects of vitamin D on glucose metabolism, insulin sensitivity, and metabolic syndrome.

Pediatric Health

Understanding how vitamin D status during development impacts long-term health outcomes in children and adolescents.
